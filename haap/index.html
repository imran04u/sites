About Us

HAAPCOL, earlier known as H. Al Hoty Establishment was founded in 1994 for provision of high quality materials testing equipment to analytical laboratories manufacturing industries.

The company successfully participated for different Saudi Aramco, SABIC and other ministerial construction projects, for the supply of testing and analytical machines. Major International producers of quality equipment from USA, EU, Far East and south Asia are represented by us.
