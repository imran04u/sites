About us

HAAPCOL, earlier known as H. Al Hoty Establishment was founded in 1994 for provision of high quality materials testing equipment to analytical laboratories manufacturing industries.

Company Profile

HAAPCOL, earlier known as H. Al Hoty Establishment was founded in 1994 for provision of high quality materials testing equipment to analytical laboratories manufacturing industries. The company successfully participated for different Saudi Aramco, SABIC and other ministerial construction projects, for the supply of testing and analytical machines.

Major International producers of quality equipment from USA, EU, Far East and south Asia are represented by us. Subsequently in the year 2007, the organization expanded to add a service sector company named HACE. In 2013, the trading and contracting companies were combined into one under the name Hani Al Hoty & Partner Co. Ltd.

Objective

Our main objective at HAAPCOL is to Provide Services with consistent quality guaranteed service at competitive costs to meet the needs of our customers.

General Iinformation

Established in 1980, HAAPCOL services have steadily grown establishing itself today as the full technical equipment and services provider in the field of Asbestos handling, Telecommunication, MEP engineering, Civil, Concrete and NDT Accessories.

An ISO 9001: 2008 Quality Management System Certified Company, HAAPCOL consists of two major operating segments; Trading and contracting services.

Trading

  • Civil Testing Equipment and components
  • Chemical Equipment and components
  • Nuclear Density Gauge
  • Telecom Equipment with active and passive components
  • NDT X-Ray Radiographic Films

Contracting

  • Asbestos Disposal and Removal
  • Telecom Survey, Design, Installation, OSP & ISP services
  • Cellular Radio Network Drive Test, Post Processing, and Optimization services
  • Mechanical, Electrical and Plumbing Services
