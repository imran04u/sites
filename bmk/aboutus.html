Based in Dammam, and sponsored by a distinguished strategic partners and supported by their the existence in the GCC area, Bait Al Mal Al Khaleeji (BMK) was established in 2008

As a closed joint stock company licensed by the Capital Market Authority (license number 37-08123) to provide Asset Management, Arranging, Advising, Custodian and Dealing as Principal.

We aim to seize the right investment opportunities in various sectors, relaying on enormous financial support from our sponsors, and experienced professional team, who's entitled with high principles: knowledge, creativity, professionalism, integrity and clear vision.

Our Values

We are in Bait Al Mal Al Khaleeji (BMK) rely on the well-established rules for creative work based on the best investment product innovation, and corporate finance solutions and provide them for the elite of discerning clients, which meet their needs and keep pace with aspirations for growth and lasting success.

BMK Mission

To be a leading investment products’ providers offering timely and quality solutions through a dedicated professional team and pioneering products and services.

BMK Vision

Providing the investment community with competent and proficient investment and advisory solutions, adding value to their investment portfolios and alternatives.
