Fund Objective

The Fund, which is an open investment fund, aims at capital growth over a long term through investment in the equity of the Saudi companies during the period of initial public offering, and in the shares listed in the Saudi Stock Market including the priority rights and the achievement of positive returns compared to the benchmark index, namely; the Saudi Stock Market Index ( TASI ), with the Possibility of cash dividends once a year after Obtaining the approval of the Fund's Board of Directors.

Main Investment Strategies

The Fund will invest in the equities listed in the Saudi Stock Market, including the rights issue, and there will be not specific proportions of the Fund's investment in single source shares, provided that these proportions will not exceed the proportion of the market value of the free shares of a company against the total market value of the Saudi Stock Market.

As the Fund may also invest in the shares of the Saudi companies during the period of initial public offering provided that the proportion of investment in the initial offerings must not exceed 30% of the total value of the Fund's assets.

As for the risk management, the Fund will diversify its investing portfolio through investing in a various range of the Saudi Market's sectors and the different stocks in each sector without focusing on a particular sector or industry.

Methods Used

Investment in stocks will be chosen based on the ascending analysis, represented by the analysis of a company's operational performance, the current and expected profits in addition to the analysis of the anticipated growth of the sector and economic indicators that may affect the performance of stocks.

The Fund Manager may maintain a maximum of 100% of the assets in cash or low- risk investment tools in the form of a short-term deposit in one or more Saudi Bank without having any maximum limit for each bank or cash Market Funds licensed by the Authority, based on a decision from the manager of the investment portfolio.

These banks will be chosen based on the credit rating a minimum of (A) category issued by the Moody's credit rating. As for the cash market funds, returns and risks will be studies and analyzed by the Fund Manager. No other banks or cash market funds outside the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia will be dealt with.

BMK IPO FUND

  • Unit Price

    SAR 13.021

  • %YTD Change

    8.76%

Fund Objective

The Fund is an open-ended Fund and will invest in Saudi listed companies. The Fund objective is to achieve capital appreciation by investing primarily in IPOs and newly listed companies in the Saudi Stock market during their first five years of listing and earning a positive return relative to the benchmark, the BMK Standard & Poor’s IPO Index. Unit holders may refer to the Fund Manager website to obtain information about the index. The Fund is entitled to invest available liquid cash in the money market Fund and instruments and IPO Funds that has been approved by the Saudi Capital Market Authority(CMA).The Fund will not distribute any dividend to unit holders. Rather, it will reinvest all dividend in the Fund, and this will be reflect in the price of the Fund units.

Main Investment Strategies

The Fund’s investments will be diversified across all sectors of the Saudi equity market without concentrating on a particular sector or industry.

The Fund will invest essentially in IPOs of the Saudi joint stock companies, rights issues for new companies and newly listed companies on the Saudi Stock Exchange during the first five years of their listing.

The Fund will invest available liquidity in a low risk/highly liquid asset such as Money market instruments issued by the Saudi entities in SAR and subject to the Saudi Arabian Monetary Agency (SAMA) oversight, CMA regulated Money market fund units and IPO Fund units with a similar strategy as the BMK IPO Fund Manager or any other manager, licensed by the CMA, and offered publicly.

Tools and techniques applied in managing the Fund

The Fund Manager relies on studies, team of analysts’ recommendations and evaluating the economic conditions of the Saudi Stock Exchange to make any investment decision. In addition to evaluating companies individually by studying IPO prospectuses, financial and legal due diligence reports for companies holding IPOs, and assessing the fair price for such units through the discounted cash flow and horizontal analysis. The decision to invest in newly listed companies will be made based on the Fundamental and technical analysis of the companies, in order to manage the Fund with a high level of efficiency and professionalism.

Minimum subscription and additional subscription

The minimum subscription and additional subscription is SAR 10,000.
